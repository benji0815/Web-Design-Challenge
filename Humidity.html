Visualizing Latitude vs Humidity

As we can observe based on the below scatter plot, we can see that as we get closer the Equator, the humidity level tends to be much higher than if we move away from it.

Most of the data is between -40 and +60 in terms of latitude and it shows humidity levels from 60 to 100%.

We can say that there is a positive correlation between the latitude and the humidity level (in %).
